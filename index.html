About EazyCheck

Our GPA Calculator is designed to simplify academic tracking for students across all departments. With a user-friendly interface, students can effortlessly enter their course titles, select corresponding grades, and input credit hours. The app computes your Grade Point Average (GPA) and determines your academic class, helping you stay informed about your progress. Whether you're evaluating a semester's performance or strategizing for future success, this tool is here to support your academic journey.
